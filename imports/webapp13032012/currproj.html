Informed by Data

Data is just getting interesting.  With the arrival of data.gov.uk there is a huge drive to open up new datasets.  Tortie hopes to identify key data and use it in new and novel ways.

Choosing the right place to live is one of the most important decision we can make.  Whilst now there is a huge amount of information available to help us it can be difficult and time consuming to navigate through it.  Tortie Locations aims to reconcile key data and simplify the process.

The Land Registry prices paid data for England and Wales has been freely available since February 2012. Tortie has produced a prices paid look-up tool as an opening project to implement and understand MAMP technology.
